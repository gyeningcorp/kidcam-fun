# Filter Assets

Place transparent PNG overlay images here for each filter.

## Required Files

### Cat Filter
- `cat_ears.png` — Ears overlay (anchored to top of head)
- `cat_whiskers.png` — Whiskers overlay (anchored to nose)
- `cat_nose.png` — Pink nose overlay (anchored to nose tip)

### Dog Filter
- `dog_ears.png` — Floppy ears (anchored to top of head)
- `dog_tongue.png` — Tongue overlay (anchored to mouth)
- `dog_nose.png` — Black nose (anchored to nose tip)

### Dinosaur Filter
- `dino_head.png` — Dinosaur head/spikes (anchored to top of head)
- `dino_scales.png` — Scale texture overlay (anchored to face)

### Princess Filter
- `princess_crown.png` — Tiara/crown (anchored to top of head)
- `princess_sparkles.png` — Sparkle overlay (full face)

### Superhero Filter
- `superhero_mask.png` — Domino mask (anchored to eyes)
- `superhero_cape_hint.png` — Cape hint at shoulders

### Pirate Filter
- `pirate_hat.png` — Pirate hat (anchored to top of head)
- `pirate_eyepatch.png` — Eye patch (anchored to right eye)

### Space Filter
- `space_helmet.png` — Space helmet (full head)
- `space_stars.png` — Background stars overlay

### Monster Filter
- `monster_horns.png` — Monster horns (anchored to top of head)
- `monster_teeth.png` — Fangs overlay (anchored to mouth)

### Rainbow Filter
- `rainbow_overlay.png` — Rainbow sparkle face overlay
- `rainbow_cheeks.png` — Rainbow cheek blush

### Snowflake Filter
- `snowflake_overlay.png` — Snowflake particles overlay
- `snowflake_crown.png` — Ice crown (anchored to top of head)

## Asset Specs
- Format: PNG with transparency
- Resolution: 512x512px minimum (will be scaled to face)
- Color space: sRGB
